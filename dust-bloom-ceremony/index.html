Dust Bloom Ceremony Browser Extension

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Dust Bloom Ceremony handles information when you apply, customize, and remove its temporary dust-particle background effect on the current browser tab.

Effective date: July 14, 2026 Last updated: July 14, 2026
Privacy summary Dust Bloom Ceremony does not collect personal information, does not track browsing activity, does not display advertising, and does not send webpage content or effect activity to external servers. Only local visual preferences are stored in the browser.

1. Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the Dust Bloom Ceremony browser extension and the visual controls available through its toolbar interface. It explains how the extension processes effect settings, what browser permissions it uses, and what information it deliberately does not collect.

Dust Bloom Ceremony provides one focused function: adding a temporary ambient background effect to the active webpage. Soft dust-like particles bloom and pulse across the page, while the user can adjust intensity, speed, and cluster size.

This policy does not govern the privacy practices of the browser, the extension marketplace, the websites being viewed, or any other software installed on the device.

2. How Dust Bloom Ceremony Works

Dust Bloom Ceremony activates only after the user invokes the extension from the toolbar. Once activated, the extension applies a temporary particle overlay to the current active tab.

The visual effect is rendered locally in the browser. The extension uses the selected intensity, speed, and cluster-size settings to control how particles appear and move.

Refreshing the page, navigating away, closing the tab, disabling the extension, or removing the effect ends the current visual session. Dust Bloom Ceremony does not preserve a remote record of where or when the effect was used.

3. Information Dust Bloom Ceremony Does Not Collect

Dust Bloom Ceremony does not collect, transmit, or maintain personal information. It is not designed to identify users or associate effect usage with a real person.

In particular, the extension does not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, telephone numbers, or account credentials;
  • Passwords, payment information, or financial details;
  • Browsing history, visited URLs, bookmarks, or search queries;
  • Website content, form entries, private messages, or files from the device;
  • IP addresses, precise location, device identifiers, or demographic information;
  • Advertising identifiers, analytics, telemetry, or behavioral profiles;
  • Effect histories, page-specific usage logs, or records of visual settings by website.

Because these categories of information are not collected, the developer does not maintain a database of identifiable users or a remote archive of effect activity.

4. Local Processing of the Visual Effect

The particle overlay, pulse timing, and cluster movement are generated locally on the active page. The extension processes only what is necessary to draw, update, and remove the requested effect.

Dust Bloom Ceremony does not analyze webpage meaning, extract text, inspect form fields, or use visual activity to infer interests or behavior.

Effect-related processing is temporary and limited to the active tab after user interaction.

5. Browser Permissions

Dust Bloom Ceremony requests only the permissions needed to apply its visible effect and remember user-selected settings.

activeTab This permission gives the extension temporary access to the currently active tab after the user invokes it. It is used only to apply the visual effect to the page the user is viewing and does not provide unrestricted background access to every site.
scripting This permission allows the extension to place, control, and remove the temporary dust-bloom overlay on the active page. It is not used to scrape websites, intercept credentials, or silently monitor browsing behavior.
storage This permission is used to remember the user's chosen intensity, speed, and cluster size for future sessions. It is not used for tracking, advertising, profiling, or collecting personal information.

6. Local Storage and User Preferences

Dust Bloom Ceremony stores only limited visual settings in the browser's local extension storage. These settings may include intensity, animation speed, cluster size, and whether the effect was enabled.

Stored preferences are used only to restore the user's chosen visual configuration. They are not uploaded to the developer, sold to third parties, or used for marketing.

Users can remove locally stored settings by resetting the extension, clearing browser extension data, or uninstalling Dust Bloom Ceremony.

7. Advertising, Analytics, and Tracking

Dust Bloom Ceremony does not display advertisements and does not contain advertising SDKs, sponsored content systems, affiliate tracking, or monetization code based on user data.

The extension does not use analytics services, tracking pixels, browser fingerprinting, session replay tools, cross-site identifiers, or behavioral profiling.

Dust Bloom Ceremony does not follow users across websites, build interest profiles, or share activity with advertisers, analytics providers, or data brokers.

8. Remote Code and External Services

Dust Bloom Ceremony does not use remote code. The executable logic and visual assets required to generate the dust-particle effect are included in the installed extension package.

The extension does not download additional executable scripts from an external server at runtime and does not depend on third-party processing services to render the effect.

The browser or extension marketplace may independently process information related to installation, updates, security, or store usage under its own privacy policy.

9. Data Sharing, Sale, and Disclosure

Dust Bloom Ceremony does not sell, rent, license, trade, or disclose personal information. It does not share webpage activity, visual preferences, or usage details with advertisers, analytics companies, data brokers, social networks, or marketing services.

Because the extension does not collect personal information or maintain remote usage records, there is ordinarily no user data available for commercial sharing or disclosure.

10. Data Security and Retention

Dust Bloom Ceremony reduces privacy risk by avoiding the collection and transmission of personal information. Visual processing occurs locally, and only limited preferences are retained in browser storage.

Local preferences remain available until changed by the user, cleared through browser settings, removed during a reset, or deleted when the extension is uninstalled.

No technical system can eliminate every possible security risk. However, the extension follows a limited-purpose design and does not maintain a developer-controlled archive of webpage activity or identifiable information.

11. Children's Privacy

Dust Bloom Ceremony does not knowingly collect personal information from children or from users of any age. The extension does not provide account registration, messaging, targeted advertising, or social networking features.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be revised if the extension's features, permissions, or information practices change. Any updated version will include a revised effective date and will describe the extension's current behavior.

If a future release introduces analytics, cloud synchronization, external processing, or additional permissions, this policy will be updated accordingly.

13. Contact

Questions, privacy inquiries, or requests for clarification may be sent to the support address listed on Dust Bloom Ceremony's official browser-extension store page.
